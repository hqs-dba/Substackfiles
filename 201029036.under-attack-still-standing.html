However, beneath the justices sits a host of judges on the federal bench. They have become the last hope for America’s future as a democracy. Despite Trump’s branding these men and women as hacks and worse when they stand up for the law, the federal bench continues to demonstrate considerable courage. A judge ordered him to remove his name from the Kennedy Center. A judge issued an order to cease White House ballroom construction. A judge froze any distribution of money from the $1.776 billion political slush fund controlled by Mr. Trump’s Department of Justice. A judge ordered him to halt his illegal tariffs. A judge declared that his executive order to end the guarantee of birthright citizenship was unconstitutional. A judge ordered the restoration of federal funds to localities. A judge ordered a freeze on illegal ICE-conducted deportations. Per the New York Times, and as of 26 May 2026, Trump’s administration has been sued no less than 650 times, in several cases by multi-state attorneys general. While this chief executive engages in lawlessness at every opportunity, he is stopped, albeit sporadically, by a judiciary that insists on upholding the laws of our land.

The struggle continues. Mr. Trump’s Department of Homeland Security was recently called out by Democrats in Congress for ignoring multiple court orders. Having lived and worked in seventeen different countries, some of which were debauched in similar fashion, I have learned that when a national leader is corrupt, he immediately corrupts the institutions that are supposed to guarantee integrity in government. I, for one, am grateful for their fortitude.
